## ðŸŽ“ Study Techniques

### For Each Question:

1. **Read the question** - Don't look at answer yet!
2. **Try to answer** - Write it down or say it out loud
3. **Read the explanation** - Compare with your answer
4. **Study the code** - Type it out, don't just read
5. **Do the challenge** - No peeking at solution!
6. **Check mastery** - Can you explain it simply?

```
- Plan tomorrow's topics
```
